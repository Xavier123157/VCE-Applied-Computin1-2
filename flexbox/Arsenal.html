Trophies

Arsenal FC have a total of 46 trophies in the clubs 136 year establishment. this tally includes:

13x League titles(including one unbeaten title)

14x FA cups the most out of any club.

2x League cups

16x Community shields

1x Centenary trophy

1x European Cup Winners' Cup

1x Inter-Cities Fairs cup

This is the 3rd highest amount of trophies in the Premiere league making them one of the greatest team in the league.

Legends

The greatest legends of the game were bred from this team, with the great success from players such as Theirry Henry, Patrick Viera, Tony Adams and Ian Wright(Arsenals all-time top goal scorer).

Arsenal have acquired two Premiere League Hall of Fame inductees in Tony Adams and the French Thierry Henry with many other former Arsenal players in the verge of breaking into this list.
